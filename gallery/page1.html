VR Pokemon Experience: Ecruteak City

Tools: Unreal Engine 4, MagicaVoxel

Based on Pokemon Silver's Ecruteak City, this VR experience immerses users into the Pokemon World, allowing them to roam around the forest, reach a pagoda, and throw Pokeballs to catch Eevee.
