Visual Identity Proposal: NYUAD Capstone Festival 2019

Tool: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

This is a visual identity proposal for NYU Abu Dhabi's annual Arts & Humanities Capstone Festival, a celebration of the senior class' year-long thesis projects, including poster sessions, readings, film screenings, art exhibitions, and theater performances.

Despite being a huge culminating event, the festival has had no coherent, recognizable identity, with a new booklet and graphics done every year. This proposal thus provides a visual identity and visual system that would be reusable and recognizable every year, to be used for the festival's advertising, the print program, each Capstone project's poster, and signage. ​​​​​​​
