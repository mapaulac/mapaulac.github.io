Poster Series: Take Care of Yourself

Tool: Adobe Photoshop

These two graphics were exhibited as A2 size posters in The Cube, a student exhibition space in New York University Abu Dhabi. They were part of the series called "Take Care of Yourself", where various artists submitted their responses to the universal feeling of rejection.
