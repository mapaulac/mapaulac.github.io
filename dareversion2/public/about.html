ABOUT

This game is one of the two main components of my Capstone project, Simulacra, which is an exploration of people's relationship with social media and their use of it.

By challenging players to complete dares of varying difficulty in their social media accounts, this game invites people to reconsider their attachment to their online personas (such as their Instagram and Facebook profiles) and have a sense of how these have the potential to unconsciously frame their own identities.

- María Paula Calderón, B.A. Interactive Media
