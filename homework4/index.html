We are MALPAIS

At the end of the Nicoya peninsula, beyond Cabo Blanco and the last road, there is the refuge of the exiles, the endless inhospitable paddock, the basalt beach where we do not know if we are going or if we return. The beach of Malpaís, Paradise.

A bad name for such a beautiful place. A contradiction. Perhaps as contradictory as the name of Costa Rica itself, which for centuries was the poorest corner of the continent.

Malpais is the place where we come from, the dry lands of the northwest, the sea that explodes, the dusty roads. From there comes our way of speaking and making these songs. And Malpaís is our idea of music: the meeting place beyond the roads.
