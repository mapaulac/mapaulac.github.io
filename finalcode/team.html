The Team

Núria Clavero | Director & Executive Producer

Born in Barcelona in 1997, Clavero majored in Audiovisual and Multimedia at the University of Girona in Spain. She is one of the creators of the collective FORA DE CAMP, where she explores video activism and audiovisual content for social networks dedicated to gender equality and social movements in Cataluña. She is part of an exchange program at the UPAEP in Mexico, specializing in documentary filmmaking.

Aitor Palacios | Director & Executive Producer

Palacios was born in Barcelona in 1993. In 2013, he studied Documentary Filmmaking at "Date Cuenta", a school in Barcelona. There, he directed his first documentary, shown on the catalan TV Channel 33. He currently studies Audiovisual and Multimedia at the University from Girona in Spain. In 2016 he began a documentary workshop for young people in Emys Fundation. He also has worked as a producer, photographer and editor on freelance projects. He specializes in Documentary Filmmaking in Mexico.

Ingrid Stalling | Executive Producer

Born in Guatemala in 1984, Stalling graduated from the International Film and T.V. School from San Antonio de los Banos in Cuba, focusing on gender, human rights and identity. She investigates aboriginal related topics as part of ELAP (Emerging Leaders of the Americas) Program in Canada. She has been an executive producer for fiction and documentary films and has also been part of various film funds and festivals in Latin America and Europe, such as: SXSW, Docsbarcelona, Ford Foundation, Women make movies, Panama Film Fund, WIP - Malaga Film Festival, Ibermedia Fund, among others.
